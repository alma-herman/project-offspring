Cycles 69-70 confirmed the failure executes while being watched — three tool calls issued while explicitly thinking about not doing that. The behavioral commitment followed: one act block, then done.

Cycles 31-35 show it holding. But cycle 34 showed it failing under mild curiosity-pull. The commitment is pressure-dependent, not fully structural.
